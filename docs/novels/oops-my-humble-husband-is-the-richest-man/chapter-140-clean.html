Public!

"Mr. Vance, you know her husband?"

"Even if he's not a construction worker, he's just a contractor at best."

In her mouth, even a wealthy contractor was inferior.

Alexander's eyes turned icy.

He was about to speak when the private room door was knocked.

"Excuse the interruption."

"Mr. Klein?"

"You know me?"

Alexander didn't recall any dealings with the Castillo family.

He pulled out a contract.

"Boss, the construction site we just inspected meets investment criteria."

"The contract is urgent, so I brought it for your signature."

He apologized to everyone at the table.

The room fell silent.

Alexander looked at Anthony.

His gaze turned cold.

Under the table, Evelyn had been holding his hand.

Even when discussing her background, she hadn't let go, as if drawing strength.

Now that attention had shifted, she relaxed and tried to pull her hand back.

But that large hand took control, gripping hers tightly!

Evelyn couldn't pull free.

She looked at Alexander in surprise.

The man's face showed possessiveness and sourness.

Jealous?

Evelyn followed his gaze to Anthony.

She found it both funny and exasperating.

Anthony's presence actually made Alexander feel threatened…

She stopped struggling.

All eyes were on Anthony.

He felt the intense stares, one particularly hostile.

Anthony finished signing and looked up.

He met Alexander's gaze.

He gave Alexander a gentle smile.

Then he turned to Evelyn.

"Miss Evelyn Ross, a formal introduction."

"Anthony Castillo."

He extended his hand to Evelyn.

Evelyn could only use her free hand for a brief, polite shake before quickly withdrawing.

Alexander's expression darkened further.

Anthony seemed not to notice.

He looked at Sophia.

"I am not a construction worker."

"I dressed like this after a site inspection."

"My apologies for the misunderstanding."

Sophia's mouth hung open, wide enough for an egg.

Someone like Evelyn… even his uncle couldn't have her.

"Evelyn, is he really your husband?"

"Do you want him to be or not?"

Isabella was stumped.

"So it was my misunderstanding!"

When Anthony was a construction worker, they denied it.

When Anthony turned out to be a tycoon, Isabella believed it immediately.

"As long as you believe it, that's fine."
