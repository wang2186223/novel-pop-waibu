Chapter 349

Chloe Roland was stunned.

She couldn't believe her ears.

She grabbed Ryan Roland's hand. "And then?"

Ryan rubbed his temples. "I tested her a few times in the car."

"She felt familiar, yet also different."

"It's hard to explain."

Chloe released his hand. "So is it her or not?"

"I'm not sure."

Chloe's eyes reddened. "Brother, are you going soft on her because she looks like our savior?"

"You must not help her anymore!"

"She is not our savior!"

Ryan sighed helplessly. "I know. I know."

"You don't understand at all!"

Chloe shook off his hand, her eyes blazing red. "You're all drawn to her just because she resembles our savior!"

"This is blasphemy against our savior!"

"She is not her!"

"Brother, I forbid you to look at her again!"

Chloe was agitated, breathing rapidly.

Ryan immediately stepped forward and gently patted her back.

He sighed deeply.

He recalled the doctor's diagnosis yesterday when he took Chloe for her appointment.

"Miss Roland's depression is severe."

"Her emotions are extremely unstable."

"She requires careful nurturing and must avoid any stimulation."

Thinking of this, Ryan softened his voice. "Alright."

"I won't look at her."

"I won't help her."

"Is that okay?"

Chloe slowly calmed down.

They entered the wedding venue together.

Ryan escorted her to the backstage lounge.

Chloe watched Ryan closely.

She was satisfied only when she saw he truly wasn't looking at Evelyn Ross.

She entered the room and sat on the sofa.

Tina Sherwood and Penelope Valentine were chatting.

They immediately surrounded her upon seeing her.

"Chloe, did you find out?"

"Why did your brother help her?"

"Is he interested in her?"

Chloe immediately refuted them. "My brother just didn't want any trouble at the wedding."

"Stop guessing wildly."

"That's good then."

Someone else chimed in. "This Grace Nan is as pretty as Mina Sherwood."

"No wonder they stick together."

"Can they do whatever they want just by relying on their looks?"

"Hmph, Mina is acceptable."

"But that Grace Nan has the face of a seductress."

"Back at the Sherwood residence, several men were looking at her with improper intentions."

Chloe's expression changed drastically. "She is not a seductress!"

"Don't you dare say that about her!"

"Her face is perfect!"

"Do not insult it!"

Their savior had the same face.

Insulting Grace Nan was insulting their savior!

The surrounding women froze, their expressions stiff.

Chloe glared at them with disgust. "No personal attacks against Grace Nan."

"Especially about her face!"

The others exchanged glances.

They walked away grudgingly.

They whispered among themselves once they were further away.

"Is Chloe Roland insane?"

"I was helping her curse someone, and she cursed me instead?"

"Don't mind her."

"She's mentally unstable."

Chloe ignored them.

She just stared blankly at Grace Nan.

Grace Nan was accompanying Mina Sherwood.

The two were whispering and laughing softly.

They didn't notice this side at all.

Grace Nan's side profile was clear.

Her peach blossom eyes had long eyelashes.

It was exactly the same as their savior.

She didn't know what was said.

But Grace Nan smiled slightly, the corners of her lips lifting.

Chloe couldn't help but curve her lips upwards too.

But the next second, Chloe snapped back to reality.

Slap!

She slapped herself hard across the face!

She wished she could slap herself a few more times.

She had actually seen a shadow of their savior in Grace Nan just now!

No!

Absolutely not!

No one in this world could replace their savior!

Chloe stood up, her eyes red.

This action stunned everyone around.

Tina Sherwood asked in confusion, "Miss Roland, what's wrong?"

Chloe said with a dark expression, "It's nothing."

She had almost done the same as Alexander Vance.

Finding a substitute!

She immediately turned her head away.

She refused to look at Evelyn Ross again.

Just then, the emcee came in to synchronize the流程.

He held the rings. "Ladies, the wedding ceremony later requires a bridesmaid and a groomsman to come on stage to deliver the rings."

"May I ask who was chosen?"

"I need to go through the流程 with the bridesmaid…"

The room fell silent instantly.

Everyone looked at Mina Sherwood simultaneously.

This was an opportunity.

All these bridesmaids were suitable due to their status.

Chloe was the cousin.

Penelope was the groom's sister.

The Sherwood girls were Mina's cousins…

Everyone thought Mina would choose from among them.

But Mina said, "It was decided long ago."

"Grace Nan will do it."

It was her wedding.

Even if it wasn't blessed, she wanted her best friend by her side.

Moreover, Mina didn't know the specifics of Grace Nan's task.

Having Grace Nan deliver the rings might help her complete her task better.

As soon as Mina finished speaking, the bridesmaids' expressions changed.

Penelope and the Sherwood women curled their lips in disdain.

Only Chloe objected vehemently. "On what basis?!"

Mina was puzzled. "What do you mean, on what basis?"

Chloe sneered. "On what basis should she go on stage to deliver the rings?"

"I disagree!"

She would never allow this person to show off using their savior's face.

Wouldn't she become even more unscrupulous in the future?

Mina frowned. "Chloe Roland, this is my wedding."

"I have the final say."

Chloe was being unreasonable. "I just disagree."

"Marcus Valentine won't agree either."

"Call him here if you don't believe me!"

Back in San Francisco, Marcus Valentine and Evelyn Ross eventually reconciled.

Marcus had repeatedly praised Evelyn's superb horseback riding skills.

He genuinely admired her…

So Chloe was certain Marcus wouldn't agree.

Mina scoffed. "Chloe Roland, it's my wedding."

"My bridesmaid."

"I don't need anyone else's agreement!"

The two were at a stalemate.

The emcee wisely retreated.

He quickly went to fetch Marcus Valentine.

Marcus entered the room impatiently. "What's all the noise about?"

Chloe immediately pointed at Evelyn Ross. "Marcus Valentine, look at Mina's best friend!"

"Mina actually wants her to go on stage to deliver the rings!"

Chloe didn't know how to explain.

She just said hatefully, "She is not worthy!"

"Marcus Valentine, she is not worthy!"

"Why is she not worthy?!" Mina pressed.

Why?

Of course because she wasn't worthy of having the same face as their savior!

But Chloe couldn't say that.

She struggled for a long time.

She suddenly looked at Penelope Valentine and Tina Sherwood beside her.

Remembering what they had said…

Although she felt it was wrong, she blurted out without thinking, "Because her status is not worthy!"

"The marriage alliance of the Five Great Families is a major event."

"The bridesmaid going on stage must come from one of the Five Great Families."

"How can you let some commoner go on stage?!"

She knew these words were wrong.

Their savior often said everyone was equal.

She never judged people by their status.

But now, Chloe was speaking recklessly.

In her extreme state, she even nodded after saying this.

"Marcus Valentine, am I right?!"
