Chapter 342

Evelyn Ross and Alexander Vance sat in the Roland family living room.

She glanced around.

The decor was warm and elegant.

"I didn't expect Chloe's home to be like this."

Alexander frowned slightly.

He looked displeased.

"Don't you feel any sense of crisis?"

Evelyn looked at him.

"What crisis?"

Alexander lowered his voice.

"Chloe Roland is coveting her friend's husband."

"She's being so blatant about it."

"You should keep your distance."

Evelyn smiled faintly.

"There must be some misunderstanding."

Alexander sighed.

Many women had pursued him over the years.

He never expected Chloe would act this way too.

Chloe was Evelyn's close friend.

He couldn't be too disrespectful.

Alexander adjusted his hair.

Then he messed up his athletic wear.

Evelyn grabbed his hand.

"Stop that."

"No matter what you do, you can't hide your handsomeness."

Alexander's ears gradually turned red.

Evelyn deliberately stared at him.

The man coughed.

"Control yourself."

"You can look all you want when we get home tonight."

Evelyn laughed.

There was movement at the door.

Ryan Roland entered with Chloe.

Ryan had lost some weight.

His gaze fell on Evelyn's face.

Alexander stepped forward to block his view.

Ryan withdrew his gaze.

Evelyn looked at Chloe.

Chloe held her chin high.

Gone was her usual gentle timidity.

Replaced by a willful air.

She stared at Evelyn the moment she entered.

Her eyes filled with hostility.

Chloe walked up to Evelyn.

She extended her hand.

"Hello, I'm Chloe Roland."

Just as Evelyn was about to shake it.

Chloe withdrew her hand and touched her head.

"You're Grace, right?"

"You resemble a friend of mine."

Evelyn remained silent.

Chloe continued.

"Want to know who?"

"Mr. Vance's deceased wife."

"Did you know about this?"

Evelyn nodded.

Chloe clenched her fist.

"You knew?"

Evelyn nodded again.

Chloe scoffed.

"You're happy being someone's substitute?"

Alexander stepped in front of Evelyn.

"Miss Roland, watch your tone."

Chloe took a step forward.

"Protecting her already?"

She stared at Alexander.

"Mr. Vance, let's be clear."

"I had my eye on you when you were with my benefactor."

"I held back out of respect for her then."

"Now she's gone."

"Why don't our families arrange a marriage?"

Alexander's eyes darkened.

He glanced back at Evelyn.

Evelyn spoke.

"Miss Roland, you shouldn't say such things."

Chloe looked at her.

"I'm not joking."

"I want him."

"If you want cooperation, give him to me."

"Otherwise, forget it!"

She turned and walked out.

Ryan looked helplessly at Alexander.

"Mr. Vance, you heard my sister."

Alexander suppressed his anger.

"You indulge her like this?"

Ryan spread his hands.

"Our parents only have this one daughter."

"She's my only sister."

"After everything that's happened."

"We have to treasure her."

Alexander looked helplessly at Evelyn.

Evelyn stepped forward.

"Mr. Roland, cooperation isn't child's play."

"Miss Roland's condition doesn't seem right."

"I suggest taking her to see a psychiatrist."

Ryan was stunned.

Chloe had become increasingly obsessive since returning home.

The family thought it was due to trauma.

He didn't expect Evelyn to notice the problem.

Could Chloe have mental issues?

Ryan pondered for a moment.

"Thank you for the reminder, Miss Nan."

"I'll take her for a check-up."

Evelyn turned and left with Alexander.

Ryan watched their retreating figures.

Alexander still walked half a step behind Evelyn.

Just like he did with Evelyn before.

Grace's mannerisms also closely resembled Evelyn's.

Very different from the submissive person described in the investigation.

Suspicion grew in Ryan's heart.

He hurried after them.

Evelyn and Alexander reached the parking lot.

Just as they were about to get in the car.

Ryan called out urgently.

"Miss Evelyn!"

Evelyn's steps faltered.
