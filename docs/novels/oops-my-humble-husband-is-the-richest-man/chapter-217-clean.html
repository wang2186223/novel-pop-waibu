Only Dr. Liam Lawrence and Fiona Vance were inside. No one else.

Mr. Valentine frowned.

Alexander looked at Fiona. "Where is Mrs. Vance?"

Fiona answered respectfully. "Miss Cecilia said a certain medicine was missing. Mrs. Vance accompanied her to purchase it."

Richard secretly sighed in relief.

The urgent matter was investigating if Dr. Lawrence's theft was connected to Dr. Frank Melville.

If Dr. Melville was involved, he could forget about remaining director!

Lost in thought, Evelyn arrived at Le Jardin Secret.

Entering, she saw Dr. Melville sitting at a public table. He waited for her bored.

Evelyn walked directly over.

Dr. Melville was around forty. His skin was pale from years of research. His slightly plump build made him appear younger.

Dr. Melville stood with a smile. "Director Nan, you've finally arrived!"

Evelyn sat across from him.

Dr. Melville handed over a box of medicine. "This is the batch you requested."

He also produced a printed document.

It was a list of cases meeting the screening criteria. Each person's physical condition was recorded in detail.

Dr. Melville said, "These are the preliminary candidates. You just need to finalize the list."

Evelyn looked at him. "Director Melville, is there a researcher surnamed Lawrence at the New York Institute?"

Dr. Melville paused. "There are several. Which one do you mean?"

Evelyn's gaze was sharp. "The one connected to the Truman family."

Dr. Melville's eyes were clear. He held nothing back. "Oh, Liam Lawrence. He's been busy at the institute recently. He just took ten days of annual leave!"

Evelyn sneered. "Do you know he's in San Francisco?"

Dr. Melville was stunned. "What? He's in San Francisco? On vacation? I truly didn't know..."

He sensed something wrong. "Director Nan, what happened?"

Evelyn laughed coldly. "Someone showed up at the Vance family. Claimed they obtained an experimental slot. Is throwing their weight around."

Dr. Melville shot to his feet. "Impossible! The slots haven't been released yet! Where did he get one?!"

Evelyn remained silent.

Cold sweat dripped down Dr. Melville's back. "Director Nan, I understand. I will investigate immediately!"

He rushed out to investigate without even eating.

Since she was here, she might as well eat.

Her seat faced the restaurant entrance. She ate while watching the scenery.

A man in a black cloak strode through the door.
