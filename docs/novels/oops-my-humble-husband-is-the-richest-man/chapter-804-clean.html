Chapter 804

Natalie Castillo blinked.

"I've seen cases like his before."

Her tone was calm.

"All the medical reports come back normal, but conception just doesn't happen. They've tried IVF. Failed every single time. Not one successful implantation."

She paused.

"The human body has a pH balance. This suggests his sperm have very specific survival requirements. The environment of a typical woman's egg cells can't meet them."

After saying this, she looked at Joanna Sterling.

"Actually, getting his consorts pregSterlingt isn't impossible."

Joanna looked up.

Natalie's lips curved.

"Just send a man in, right?"

Joanna's mouth twitched.

"You think they're fools? You might hide it for the first three months, but after that, they'll definitely run a DNA test. It's impossible to conceal. Besides, those consorts serve their king willingly. They wouldn't betray him."

Natalie raised an eyebrow.

"So, what's the solution?"

Joanna was silent for a moment.

She sighed.

"I heard their king secretly came to the country recently, wanting to try traditional medicine. That's why I thought we had a chance. If we can cure his condition, I can go back."

Natalie immediately looked at Evelyn Ross.

"They're here? When?"

Joanna spoke.

"They arrived a few days ago. Their movements are very discreet. But... there's one person who should know."

Natalie's eyes lit up.

They spoke in unison.

"Sebastian Truman."

Sebastian Truman's position was special.

He was involved in diplomatic exchanges at the national level.

He likely knew the king's whereabouts.

Otherwise, they wouldn't dare act recklessly.

Casually inquiring about such a figure's movements could easily get them labeled as spies if discovered.

To meet this king, to diagnose him, they truly needed Sebastian's introduction.

Evelyn wasn't skilled in diagnosis.

She only knew how to make medicine.

She looked at Natalie.

"How's your medical skill?"

Natalie lifted her chin.

"Just average. Third best in the world."

Evelyn was speechless.

That smug look was almost unbearable.

She smiled helplessly.

After thinking for a moment, she still picked up her phone.

She knew troubling Sebastian like this wasn't ideal.

Especially an introduction at this level.

But she wasn't a saint.

Not using available connections and foolishly trying to approach the target herself wasn't nobility. It was stupidity.

Evelyn made the call.

She explained her purpose.

She added a final remark.

"If it's inconvenient, just forget it."

She wouldn't pressure anyone.

Sebastian paused.

He laughed.

"Miss Ross, between us, there's no need for such formality. You saved my life. Besides, this is a small matter... King Montgomery and I hit it off immediately. We're already friends. My father happened to assign his medical visit to me. You're actually helping me by introducing a renowned doctor."

His voice was gentle.

"How about this? Let's meet at the club tomorrow, alright?"

Evelyn immediately agreed.

"Alright. See you tomorrow."

After hanging up, she looked at Joanna.

Joanna sighed.

"This is the benefit of connections."

Natalie stroked her chin.

"I just wonder if the jealous one will get upset?"

As she said this, she tilted her chin towards behind Evelyn.

Evelyn immediately turned her head.

Alexander Vance was standing behind her, watching her quietly.

Evelyn raised an eyebrow.

Alexander smiled at her, then turned and left.

Joanna said enviously, "Men as loyal as Mr. Vance are rare, right? Men change when they get money. They're all creatures of instinct. For him to be so restrained is truly remarkable."

Evelyn stood up and walked out.

"My fifth brother is also very loyal."

Joanna was speechless.

Time quickly moved to the next day.

Evelyn proactively invited Alexander to go to the club together.

Alexander drove.

When they arrived, he didn't get out of the car.

"The Truman family's club is a top-level secure facility. Safety isn't a concern. I won't go in. I'll wait for you outside."

Evelyn was puzzled.

"You're not coming in?"

Alexander took out his laptop.

"I have a meeting."

"Alright."

Evelyn, Joanna, and Natalie got out of the car.

The three of them walked towards the club entrance.

There was a security checkpoint at the door.

The procedure was strict, the inspection thorough.

The three soon entered.

Just inside, they saw Elian Cross walking over with his young attendant.

Seeing Evelyn, Elian's eyes brightened.

"Miss Ross! What are you doing here?"

Evelyn also looked at him curiously.

"What are you doing here?"

Elian smiled slightly.

"I heard the food here is excellent. I came to try. But it still didn't work."

He looked dejected.

"If it had worked, I wouldn't need to trouble you, Miss Ross."

Evelyn shook her head.

"It's fine."

Elian was tactful.

"You have business, right? I won't disturb you."

"Alright."

Evelyn nodded to him and continued walking further into the club.

They soon reached the private room Sebastian mentioned.

She knocked on the door.

After a moment, Sebastian himself opened it.

"King Montgomery doesn't want to see outsiders, so there aren't many people inside."

Evelyn nodded.

After entering the room, she looked at Montgomery.

At this glance, she was slightly taken aback.

Montgomery looked strangely familiar.
