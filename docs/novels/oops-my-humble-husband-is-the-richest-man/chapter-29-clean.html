They are mother and daughter.

These words made all three people in the room look at her simultaneously.

Isabella was stunned. "Where is Dr. Nan?"

Julian also looked over urgently.

Isabella's eyes welled up with tears. "Julian, what should we do?"

She had no acquaintances at San Francisco General... Would she have to ask that person for help?

Evelyn stood frozen in place.

Though worried about Mrs. Ashcroft's condition, she understood she had no right to step through that door.
