Chapter 402

Alexander Vance was always aloof and rarely close to anyone.

The person speaking with him now was a childhood friend, so he took the question very seriously.

He answered directly, "No rush. I'll introduce you to her when she arrives."

Everyone burst into laughter.

"Alright, keeping it so mysterious! We're all waiting for your good news!"

At the entrance, Penelope Valentine overheard this and glanced at her other brothers.

She noticed their expressions were grim.

She immediately moved closer to Ethan Valentine and whispered, "Third Brother, does everyone dislike Grace?"

Ethan snorted coldly, "Of course!"

Penelope asked eagerly, "Is it because she kept a boy toy after her divorce?"

"Exactly!"

Ethan said with a dark expression, "You even know about that?"

"Of course I know!" Penelope looked innocent.

Ethan sneered, "How could she do this to Third Uncle?"

Penelope: ?

She stared blankly at Ethan, her mind full of questions.

Why would it be wrong toward Third Uncle?

Did it mean she should have sought Third Uncle's permission before dating someone?

Thinking this, Penelope quickly nodded, "Right, she's so wrong to Third Uncle!"

Ethan exchanged looks with his other brothers.

Then he turned to Marcus Valentine, "Big Brother, you handle the guests here. We'll go entertain inside. Many people have already arrived."

Marcus didn't overthink it and simply nodded, "Go ahead."

This relaxed the six brothers, and they walked straight into the banquet hall.

Ethan first looked in the direction of Evelyn Ross and Mina Sherwood.

Both were keeping a low profile.

Evelyn even wore a black evening gown and stood quietly in a corner.

Charles Valentine couldn't help saying, "See her? She's quite pretty, and somehow seems very approachable. How could she do this to Third Uncle?"

Ethan sneered, "Appearances can be deceptive. That's just how she is! At Cortez Sect, I was almost fooled by her!"

The other brothers chimed in:

"What should we do now?"

"Talk to her? Either leave that boy toy or leave this banquet hall?"

"Good idea. We can't let Third Uncle become a laughingstock in New York!"

Charles suddenly said, "I have an immature idea!"

Everyone immediately looked at him.

Charles raised an eyebrow, "What if I recognize her as my adopted younger sister? Then Third Uncle can't introduce her as his wife, right?"

Ethan's eyes lit up, "Brilliant! Why didn't I think of that!"

"This family reunion gala would have a proper purpose then!"

"But would she agree to be your adopted sister?"

Charles said immediately, "She'll definitely agree. Being the Valentine family's adopted daughter comes with many benefits!"

He then ran over to Evelyn.

Evelyn was holding a plate, lingering at the buffet area.

Banquets always had this downside—she never felt full.

The shelves were full of fancy but impractical things...

Evelyn picked up a cookie and turned to find Mina.

Instead, she found Charles standing before her, hands in pockets, sneering, "I ask you, will you be my adopted sister or not?"

Evelyn: ??

She raised an eyebrow and followed Charles's gaze to where the five brothers, led by Ethan, stood.

Then she chuckled softly and said calmly, "Alright."

Charles's eyes brightened, "I knew you'd make the right choice! Later, you tell Third Uncle this news. Don't let him announce you as his wife, understand?"

Charles didn't look much older than her.

His eyes held a naive innocence.

Evelyn realized the Valentine boys were quite simple-minded...

The Valentine family upbringing was good.

She lowered her eyes, "Fine."

Charles was surprised by her quick agreement, "You mean it?"

"Yes."

"Good, it's settled then!"

Charles ran back excitedly.

Evelyn waved her plate at the six brothers and continued lingering near the food.

"How did it go?"

"How was it!"

As soon as Charles returned, everyone crowded around to ask.

Charles told them about Evelyn's reaction, and everyone was thrilled, "That's great!"

Charles added, "Third Brother, I think Grace is quite nice. When we talked, her smile was very cute!"

Ethan sneered, "Silly boy, don't be fooled by her appearance!"

The group laughed and joked...

After eating a few cookies, Evelyn wanted more but was stopped by Mina, "There's still the formal dinner later. If you eat so many cookies now, won't you have room for the meal?"

Evelyn paused, "There's still a dinner?"

Mina nodded and pointed to a private room, "This is just the buffet area. There's a formal banquet..."

Then she pointed to the head table, "You're Third Uncle's daughter and today's main guest. You'll sit at the head table later."

Evelyn: "........"

She thought these cookies were dinner.

So high-society banquets were this elaborate!

Evelyn waited a while longer as others gradually moved toward the banquet tables.

Evelyn went with Mina first to see her to the secondary table.

As Marcus's wife, Mina had a seat there.

Evelyn then looked at the name cards to find her own seat.

As she turned, she saw the Valentine group approaching.

Penelope was among them with Ethan.

Seeing Evelyn, Penelope immediately looked at Ethan, "Third Brother, is Grace's seat at the head table tonight?"

Everyone immediately looked at Evelyn.

Even Penelope clenched her fingers.

She feared that once Evelyn returned home, she would surpass her as the Valentine family's most esteemed daughter.

So she was setting a trap.

She wanted Ethan to announce Evelyn's seating publicly.

Ethan looked at Evelyn, sneered, and said directly, "Of course not. The head table is for elders. As for Grace..."

Ethan looked around the venue... He was in charge of guest reception and seating arrangements today.

He immediately pointed to the third table, "You sit there! That seat suits your status!"

Mina frowned, "Third Brother, how can you arrange it like this?"
