Chapter 408

Zack Davis stared intently at Evelyn Ross.

Unemployed for days, he had been drowning his sorrows in alcohol, looking utterly disheveled.

Upon learning that NanTech Ltd. had miraculously recovered and even partnered with the Ashcroft and Sherwood families, he had repeatedly tried to reconcile.

Unfortunately, security always stopped him at the gate.

A few times, he had sneaked into the neighborhood late at night, only to be knocked unconscious and thrown out just as he approached Grace's residence.

Fortunately, the weather had turned warmer, or he would have frozen to death on the streets.

From that moment, he realized Grace had someone incredibly powerful backing her.

Until a few days ago, when someone approached him, claiming Grace was actually a love child of the Ashcroft family!

He was speechless with shock.

How was that possible?

Wasn't she an orphan? How did she suddenly become a wealthy heiress?

The person gave him an address and told him to see for himself today.

He and his mother were quietly led into the banquet by a waiter.

Finally, he saw Grace again.

She was still radiant and stunningly beautiful, her face so captivating it made him regret his past actions deeply.

How could he have ever found such a woman boring and been swayed by wildflowers outside?

He must have been completely blind!

So when his mother rushed forward, he didn't stop her. He wanted to reconcile too.

Unexpectedly, before his mother could even touch Grace's clothes, bodyguards suddenly appeared and restrained her.

Two bodyguards stared coldly at him, as if daring him to make a move.

These intimidating bodyguards, whom he wouldn't have dared to look at in the past, were now protecting Grace.

Zack forced an ingratiating smile. "Grace, it's me. Let me come over and talk, okay?"

Evelyn only responded with a cold laugh.

Zack's mother shouted again, "Grace, you can't abandon me! I'm starving and freezing at Kelsey Lopez's place. I'm your mother!"

Evelyn replied coldly, "I'm divorced from Zack. What kind of mother are you to me?"

Mrs. Davis immediately shrieked, "Now that you've climbed the social ladder, you deny us? Then we'll find Chloe. I'm her grandmother, and this is her father. She has an obligation to support us!"

Evelyn found this woman utterly shameless.

She sneered, "Chloe is only three years old. You want her to support you? Fine. Live until she's eighteen and can support herself first!"

Mrs. Davis trembled with rage.

She struggled against the bodyguards. "Grace, don't be ungrateful! Tell them to let us go immediately and allow us to join the banquet!"

She then shouted at Mr. Valentine, "In-law, we're family!"

Mr. Valentine: "..."

He found this utterly humiliating.

He didn't know how Grace had endured these two in the past.

He lowered his eyes to conceal his fury.

The real Grace was already dead. He didn't know the exact details.
