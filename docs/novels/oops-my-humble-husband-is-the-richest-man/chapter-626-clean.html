Chapter 626

Deputy Director Marcus Wright stepped forward with a smile.

Older people understood that pride was worthless compared to tangible benefits.

But Leo Wright immediately spoke up.

"Dad, you can't!

Your injuries..."

"I'm fine."

Marcus Wright cut him off directly.

"Besides, it should be an old-timer like me who sets the example.

That way, everyone else will be willing to go all out!"

He looked at the boxing champion, King.

"Let's begin."

The people around them stepped back two paces, clearing the area.

Evelyn Ross watched the arena.

If Ethan Valentine and Leo Wright could win this match, she wanted to conserve her strength.

After all, she was currently in a confrontation with the Fox.

Whoever revealed more of their hand would lose faster.

Her identity as the First Senior Sister of the Cortez Sect could not be exposed unless absolutely necessary.

She looked at Marcus Wright.

At this moment, a trace of admiration arose in her eyes.

Although her impressions of Marcus Wright from their previous meetings hadn't been good, after today's interaction, she suddenly understood why Elder Benjamin Wright had entrusted the Special Operations Division to him.

This man had courage and wasn't afraid of losing face.

To lead an organization like the Special Operations Division, you needed someone like him.

Someone who could navigate both the light and the dark, who wasn't too rigid, and whose methods to achieve goals couldn't be too fastidious at times...

As Evelyn thought this, she felt as if she had learned something from Marcus Wright...

While she was pondering, Marcus Wright had already charged at King.

King threw a direct punch.

Marcus Wright bent at the waist.

But due to his beer belly and the lumbar muscle strain in his lower back, his bending motion was a bit slow.

He was directly hit on his left arm by King!

Marcus Wright retreated two steps, steadying himself.

Leo Wright exclaimed, "Dad!"

Marcus Wright waved his hand with a smile.

"I'm fine."

He moved his shoulder, his expression still relaxed.

But Evelyn keenly noticed that cold sweat had already beaded on his forehead.

She couldn't help but walk up behind Leo Wright and ask in a low voice, "Does the old man have old injuries?"

Leo Wright glanced at her.

He disliked her for helping Grace Lorenz and ignored her.

Instead, Grace spoke up.

"Yes.

Over the years, Deputy Director Wright has carried out many secret missions for the Special Operations Division.

He has hundreds of scars all over his body.

Just serious injuries alone number seventy-eight.

There are over a dozen wounds on his back, and many cuts near his heart.

Several times, bullets grazed his heart as they went in..."

Grace's words made Leo Wright's chin lift slightly.

He felt immensely proud.

For them, these were their medals and proof!

Grace continued.

"Deputy Director Wright's waist was once broken.

During a mission, he fell from a cliff and was caught by tree branches.

Although he luckily survived, the muscles in his waist were severed and couldn't be repaired.

Since then, Deputy Director Wright has transferred to a desk job..."

Evelyn took a deep breath.

Her heart felt another surge of respect for Marcus Wright.

Leo Wright snorted coldly.

"Now you know.

Every single scar on my father's body represents his contributions.

And yet, your grandfather stubbornly refuses to step down from his position.

It's simply outrageous!"

After saying this, he turned his worried gaze back to the arena.

Evelyn's jaw tightened.

At this moment, she suddenly understood the anger of Marcus Wright and Leo Wright.

Marcus Wright was such a man.

Leo Wright must have also accomplished a great deal.

But Elder Benjamin Wright, who had clearly promised to hand over the Special Operations Division to Marcus Wright, was still, at the advanced age of eighty, clinging to his position and refusing to retire...

Anyone in that situation would harbor resentment.

Marcus Wright was only fifty, right at the age to achieve great things, yet he was being firmly suppressed by Elder Benjamin Wright.

Over time, how could resentment not grow?

But judging by Grace's demeanor, Elder Benjamin Wright definitely didn't mean it intentionally.

There must be some reason that couldn't be spoken of openly...

The Special Operations Division had been turned into a disorganized mess by the two sides.

It seemed she needed to find an opportunity to resolve the conflict between them.

After all, the Special Operations Division needed to be united to resist external enemies.

Thinking this, Evelyn moved closer to Grace and asked quietly, "Did your grandfather really not intend to hand over the Special Operations Division to Deputy Director Wright?"

Grace shook her head.

"I've never heard of such a thing.

I only ever heard Grandpa say one thing: that we should obey Deputy Director Wright's leadership in the future.

Grandpa absolutely did not have that intention."

Evelyn nodded.

While they were talking, inside the arena, Marcus Wright and King had exchanged several more rounds.

Marcus Wright, worthy of the former top fighter of the Special Operations Division.

Despite his serious injuries, he still managed to dodge several of King's attacks with nimble movements.

But just as the Special Operations Division members were being inspired by Marcus Wright, Evelyn saw the head of the International Division give King a meaningful look.

King immediately changed the force of his punch.

He threw a punch at Marcus Wright.

This punch was fast.

If it hit Marcus Wright, he might be crippled!!

Evelyn's pupils contracted.

She instinctively wanted to rush forward.

But the distance was too great; there was no time.

Leo Wright also saw this and cried out, "Dad!"

Marcus Wright clearly sensed the danger too.

He wanted to dodge, but his old waist injury made his movements sluggish.

King's fist was already right in front of him.

Marcus Wright gritted his teeth, preparing to take the punch head-on.

At that moment, a figure suddenly rushed out from the side.

It was Ethan Valentine!

He had been observing from the sidelines, and seeing Marcus Wright in danger, he rushed forward without hesitation.

Ethan Valentine stood in front of Marcus Wright, crossing his arms to block King's punch.

Thud!

A dull sound.

Ethan Valentine was forced back three steps, his arms trembling slightly.

But he held his ground.

A flicker of surprise passed through King's eyes.

He clearly hadn't expected Ethan Valentine to block his punch.

Marcus Wright breathed a sigh of relief and looked at Ethan Valentine.

"Thank you."

Ethan Valentine shook his head.

"It's what I should do."

He looked at King, his gaze sharp.

"Next, it's my turn."

King sneered.

"Interesting."

The two immediately engaged in combat.

Evelyn watched the arena, her brow furrowed slightly.

She knew Ethan Valentine's strength.

Although good, he was still somewhat inferior to King.

Fighting like this, Ethan Valentine would inevitably lose.

She looked at Leo Wright.

Leo Wright also saw this, his expression grave.

Evelyn said quietly to Grace, "Go prepare.

If Ethan loses, you're up next."

Grace was startled.

"Me?"

"Yes." Evelyn nodded.

"You are Elder Wright's granddaughter.

The Special Operations Division members will support you.

Moreover, your strength isn't weak; you should be able to hold out for a while."

Grace bit her lip.

"Alright."

She turned to prepare.

In the arena, the fight between Ethan Valentine and King had reached a fever pitch.

Ethan Valentine was clearly at a disadvantage.

His attacks didn't cause much damage to King, while every punch from King was difficult for him to parry.

Finally, King seized an opening and punched Ethan Valentine in the chest.

Ethan Valentine was sent flying backward, landing heavily on the ground.

He struggled to get up but spat out a mouthful of blood.

Leo Wright immediately rushed forward to support him.

"Ethan!"

Ethan Valentine shook his head.

"I'm fine."

But he couldn't stand up.

King looked at the Special Operations Division members.

"Next."

The Special Operations Division members exchanged glances.

Marcus Wright was injured, Ethan Valentine was defeated, and Leo Wright's strength was inferior to Ethan's...

Was the Special Operations Division really going to lose today?

Just then, Grace stepped forward.

She looked at King.

"I'll go."

King raised an eyebrow.

"You?"

"Yes." Grace took a deep breath.

"I am Elder Wright's granddaughter, Grace Lorenz."

Seeing her, a glimmer of hope flashed in the eyes of the Special Operations Division members.

Grace was young, but her strength wasn't weak.

And she represented Elder Wright.

King laughed.

"Interesting.

Let's go then."

The two immediately began fighting.

Grace's strength was indeed good.

She nimbly dodged King's attacks and occasionally counterattacked.

But Evelyn could see that Grace wouldn't last long.

King's strength was too great.

Sure enough, before long, Grace was punched in the shoulder by King and staggered back.

King pressed his advantage, throwing another punch at Grace's chest.

Grace wanted to dodge, but it was too late.

Evelyn's eyes narrowed.

She couldn't wait any longer.

Just as she was about to rush forward, a figure suddenly rushed in from the entrance.

That person was extremely fast, instantly reaching Grace and blocking King's punch.

Thud!

King took a step back, a flicker of shock in his eyes.

That person stood firmly in place, looking at King.

"What kind of skill is it to bully a young girl?"

Everyone looked at that person.

It was a middle-aged man, tall and imposing, with a stern face.

He wore a black martial arts outfit, his aura imposing.

Seeing him, Marcus Wright's eyes lit up with joy.

"Old Benjamin!"

Grace was also stunned.

"Grandpa?"

The newcomer was none other than Elder Benjamin Wright.

He looked at Grace, a trace of heartache in his eyes.

"Are you alright?"

Grace shook her head.

"I'm fine."

Only then did Elder Benjamin Wright look at King.

"I'll fight you."

King sneered.

"Elder Wright?

I heard you are the former head of the Special Operations Division, very strong.

Perfect, I'd like to learn from you."

Elder Benjamin Wright didn't waste words and immediately attacked.

His movements were extremely fast, his fist strikes fierce and sharp.

King didn't dare be careless and responded with full force.

The two fought, evenly matched.

Evelyn watched the arena, surprised in her heart.

Elder Benjamin Wright's strength was even greater than she had imagined.

Moreover, his moves were special, seeming to blend multiple martial arts styles.

Although King was powerful, he actually seemed somewhat overwhelmed in front of Elder Benjamin Wright.

Finally, Elder Benjamin Wright seized an opportunity and struck King's chest with his palm.

King retreated three steps, a trickle of blood seeping from the corner of his mouth.

He looked at Elder Benjamin Wright, a trace of wariness in his eyes.

"As expected of Elder Wright.

I admit defeat."

Elder Benjamin Wright withdrew his hand and looked at the head of the International Division.

"You've lost."

The head of the International Division's face was ugly.

But he had to admit that Elder Benjamin Wright's strength was indeed formidable.

He gritted his teeth.

"Let's go."

The International Division members left in disgrace.

The Special Operations Division members cheered.

Marcus Wright walked up to Elder Benjamin Wright, his expression complex.

"Old Benjamin, why did you come?"

Elder Benjamin Wright looked at him and sighed.

"If I didn't come, the Special Operations Division would fall apart."

Marcus Wright fell silent.

Elder Benjamin Wright looked at everyone.

"I know you have grievances against me.

You think I'm old and still clinging to my position, refusing to step down.

But today, I want to tell you, the reason I haven't stepped down is because the Special Operations Division is currently facing a huge crisis."

Everyone was stunned.

Elder Benjamin Wright continued.

"This crisis concerns the survival of the Special Operations Division.

I cannot step down, and I dare not.

Because once I do, the Special Operations Division might be finished."

Marcus Wright frowned.

"What crisis?"

Elder Benjamin Wright looked at Evelyn Ross.

"This matter is related to Miss Ross."

Evelyn was taken aback.

"Related to me?"

Elder Benjamin Wright nodded.

"Yes.

Miss Ross, come with me.

I have something to tell you."

Evelyn nodded and followed Elder Benjamin Wright away.

The two came to a quiet room.

Elder Benjamin Wright closed the door and looked at Evelyn.

"Miss Ross, I know you are the First Senior Sister of the Cortez Sect."

Evelyn's pupils contracted.

"How do you know?"

Elder Benjamin Wright smiled.

"Not only do I know you are the First Senior Sister of the Cortez Sect, I also know you are in a confrontation with the Fox."

Evelyn looked at him warily.

"Who exactly are you?"

Elder Benjamin Wright sighed.

"I am the former head of the Special Operations Division, and also a Registered Disciple of the Cortez Sect."

Evelyn was stunned.

"You are a Registered Disciple of the Cortez Sect?"

"Yes." Elder Benjamin Wright nodded.

"Back then, my master accepted me as a Registered Disciple, taught me martial arts, and tasked me with guarding the Special Operations Division.

All these years, I have been secretly investigating the Fox and discovered they are planning a huge conspiracy."

Evelyn frowned.

"What conspiracy?"

Elder Benjamin Wright's expression turned grave.

"They want to control the entire martial arts world of Aldoria, and through the martial arts world, control all of Aldoria."

Evelyn drew a sharp breath.

"How is that possible?"

"It is possible." Elder Benjamin Wright said.

"The Fox's strength is formidable.

They have already infiltrated many martial arts sects.

If they succeed, the consequences would be unimaginable."

Evelyn was silent for a moment.

"Then why didn't you tell Deputy Director Wright?"

Elder Benjamin Wright smiled bitterly.

"I couldn't tell him.

Because there are people from the Fox around Deputy Director Wright."

Evelyn was shocked.

"What?"

Elder Benjamin Wright nodded.

"Yes.

The Fox's people have already infiltrated the upper echelons of the Special Operations Division.

If I told Deputy Director Wright, the plan would be exposed.

So, I could only pretend to refuse to step down and investigate secretly."

Evelyn understood.

"So, you never stepped down to mislead the Fox?"

"Yes." Elder Benjamin Wright said.

"But I didn't expect Deputy Director Wright to develop resentment towards me because of this.

Today's events made me realize we can't continue like this.

I must tell you the truth."

Evelyn asked, "What do we do now?"

Elder Benjamin Wright looked at her.

"Miss Ross, I need your help.

You are the First Senior Sister of the Cortez Sect, powerful, and have a grudge against the Fox.

We can join forces to deal with the Fox."

Evelyn nodded.

"Alright."

Elder Benjamin Wright breathed a sigh of relief.

"Thank you, Miss Ross."

Evelyn shook her head.

"No need to thank me.

This is for myself as well."

The two discussed the next steps of the plan.

Then, Elder Benjamin Wright brought Evelyn back to the main hall.

Marcus Wright and the others were still waiting.

Elder Benjamin Wright looked at Marcus Wright, his expression serious.

"Deputy Director Wright, I have something to tell you."

Marcus Wright nodded.

"Alright."

The two went to another room.

Evelyn looked at Grace and said quietly, "Your grandfather told me the truth."

Grace was startled.

"What truth?"

Evelyn told her what Elder Benjamin Wright had said.

After listening, Grace's eyes reddened.

"So Grandpa never stepped down to protect the Special Operations Division... I misunderstood him."

Evelyn patted her shoulder.

"It's not too late to know now."

After a while, Elder Benjamin Wright and Marcus Wright came out.

Marcus Wright's expression was complex.

He walked up to Evelyn Ross and bowed deeply.

"Miss Ross, I'm sorry.

I misunderstood you before."

Evelyn shook her head.

"It's alright."

Marcus Wright looked at everyone and said loudly, "From today onward, the Special Operations Division will fully cooperate with Miss Ross to deal with the Fox!"

Although the Special Operations Division members didn't understand what had happened, seeing Marcus Wright and Elder Benjamin Wright reconcile, they all cheered.

Evelyn looked at this scene and felt relieved in her heart.

The Special Operations Division was finally united.

Next, it was time to deal with the Fox.

She looked out the window, her gaze firm.

Fox, it's time to settle the score between us.
