[Sorry, I'll only go if my mom agrees.]

"My mom said I can't play with fools."

She finally had a real mother.

She struggled to form the words.

"Mom…"

As she spoke.

Sophia, who had been completely still.

Her eyeballs shifted slightly.

Evelyn was about to turn and check.

Her phone suddenly rang.

She glanced at the caller ID.

Then looked at Sophia.

She took the phone to the outer room to answer.

She didn't notice.

"I won't show favor to anyone."

"I have someone very important in San Francisco."

"I'm curious."

"This project was shelved for so long."

"Who made you suddenly pick it back up?"

"My husband's grandmother."

"OK, understood!"

With medication, her memory should return quickly.

Why she and Alexander got married!

Mom.

"What? Grandma fainted? I'm coming right now!"

She walked to his side without hesitation.

"I'll go back with you."

Because Sophia had something to say before fainting.

Now she knew what it was.

Sophia wouldn't wake up soon.

To go back with Alexander to see Grandma!

His uncle doted on her so much.

If she just said a few nice words.

Being adopted as a goddaughter would be natural!

This Evelyn.

Really didn't know what was good for her!

"What is it?"
