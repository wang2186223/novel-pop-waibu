Chapter 769

Natalie Castillo whipped her head around.

"Where?"

Evelyn Ross's gaze snapped toward Liam Cooper.

Her voice was firm and decisive.

"He called Mia Truman. It seemed random, but there's one person who could have influenced his actions!"

Before the words had fully left her mouth, she was already striding toward Liam.

She fixed him with a sharp stare.

"The day you contacted Mia Truman, was Chloe Raymond with you?"

Liam was taken aback.

"Yes... We were practically inseparable..."

Evelyn's eyes lifted instantly, meeting Natalie's.

Natalie's pupils contracted.

"You're saying... Chloe Raymond is the mastermind behind this?"

"Where is she?"

Evelyn immediately turned to Mia Truman and Sebastian Truman.

Sebastian looked toward the manager who had just stepped out—he was the one who had taken Chloe away.

The manager hurriedly said, "Locked in the room next door. I'll go—"

The group followed the manager, rushing toward the room where Chloe was being held.

Sebastian pushed the door open!

Thick smoke billowed out!

Several people instantly covered their mouths and noses.

Alexander Vance moved to shield Evelyn behind him, his sharp eyes scanning the interior.

The room was in disarray.

Two girls who had been in the room with Chloe were unconscious on the floor.

Chloe Raymond herself—was nowhere to be seen!

The window was wide open.

The group waved away the smoke and rushed to the window.

Evelyn caught sight of fresh footprints in the muddy ground outside.

Without a word, she leaped out the window, following the trail at a sprint!

Time was critical. There was still a chance!

As soon as Evelyn moved, Natalie started to follow.

But Alexander had already vaulted through the window ahead of her.

The window was narrow, only allowing one person through at a time.

By the time Alexander had jumped out, Natalie tried to go next.

But Sebastian actually slipped past her!

Natalie: "..."

By the time she finally climbed out the window, the three of them had already vanished deep into the alleyways!

Natalie stamped her foot in frustration.

What are you two getting involved for? I'm Evelyn's primary partner!

Evelyn sprinted at full speed through the narrow alleyways.

Ahead, Chloe Raymond's fleeing figure was intermittently visible.

The back alleys behind the bar were a tangled maze.

At this hour, there were few pedestrians.

That fleeing figure was moving with alarming ease.

Evelyn pushed herself faster.

But the distance was increasing...

The branching paths grew more numerous.

A few more turns, and she might lose her target completely.

She stopped at a crossroads.

Chloe's figure had disappeared from view ahead.

Just as she hesitated, Alexander and Sebastian caught up.

Sebastian, who usually seemed so detached, ran with speed that matched Alexander's effortlessly.

His breathing was even, his expression unchanged.

His physical fitness was astonishingly good.

Evelyn decisively pointed to two of the branching paths.

"You two take those! I'll go this way!"

Splitting up to intercept, they were sure to cut her off.

Alexander silently pulled something from his coat and pressed it into her palm.

Then, without looking back, he charged down the path ahead.

Evelyn looked down.

In her hand was a compact handgun!

She quickly concealed it, glancing around.

Just as she was about to start running, Sebastian spoke up.

"Miss Ross."

Evelyn turned back.

"Can't you keep up?"

Sebastian: "..."

A man can't admit to that.

A slight twitch touched Sebastian's lips.

He stepped forward and placed something in her other hand.

Evelyn looked down.

Another elegant, small pistol.

She looked up, stunned.

Sebastian had already turned and was running down the other branching path.

The man called back over his shoulder as he ran, his voice carried by the wind.

"Strict upbringing. Military-style. Miss Ross, be careful."

With that, he disappeared around the alley corner.

Evelyn: "..."

She looked at the guns in her left and right hands.

She quickly stowed them in her pockets and launched herself into another sprint!

She couldn't lose Chloe Raymond!

She was very likely the key to unlocking the Sterling Family's secrets!

The thought flashed through her mind.

She increased her speed again.

After chasing down three more streets, she finally caught another glimpse of that panicked figure ahead!

She'd caught up!

Evelyn's eyes lit up.

She lunged forward, her fingers hooking like claws onto the other person's shoulder!
