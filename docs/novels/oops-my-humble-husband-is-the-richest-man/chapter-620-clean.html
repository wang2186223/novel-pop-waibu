Chapter 620

The expressions of the people around Benjamin Wright instantly changed.

Evelyn Ross had initially suspected Marcus Wright and his son.

But she quickly ruled them out.

When the Special Operations Division selected its successor, they must have conducted the strictest background checks.

Marcus Wright's father had sacrificed his life on a mission years ago. His family background was clean, and his loyalty was beyond question.

Just yesterday, Benjamin had mentioned that after Marcus's father's sacrifice, it was Benjamin's own father who had personally raised Marcus. He had even intended to hand over the Special Operations Division to him long ago.

That was precisely why Benjamin hadn't joined the Special Operations Division, while Marcus had become the Deputy Director.

It was just that over the years, for some unknown reason, Marcus and Benjamin's father had gradually grown distant...

After Benjamin finished speaking earlier, Marcus had fallen silent.

This indicated he wasn't truly concerned about whether Nicole Sterling was taken by the International Division.

Evelyn completely eliminated this father and son from suspicion.

Her gaze shifted to the first person who had spoken from Marcus's side—Zhang Tai.

He was the one who had ignited the conflict between the two factions.

Those who echoed him had been stirred up by his words.

This person was very likely the fox's inside man!

She then looked behind Benjamin.

The first to speak had been Grace Lorenz's little follower, the one who had gone to her house to arrest Nicole Sterling last time.

That statement had seemed to support the Wright family, but in reality, it had pushed the conflict to its peak.

This little follower was also an inside man!

Perhaps the rift between Benjamin's father and Marcus had been secretly instigated by these two inside agents.

The fox... was truly cunning.

Placing one inside agent in the Special Operations Division was difficult enough. She had managed to place two.

Having the two inside agents stir up conflict, causing internal division within the Special Operations Division, weakened the Division's power while also diminishing the presence of the International Department...

Evelyn couldn't help but admire the strategy.

As she was thinking, the scene had already descended into chaos.

Leo Wright argued until his eyes were red. "So the Wright family never had good intentions! You want my father to work for you, while also wanting your own son to inherit the position! If that's the case, why pretend to be good people in front of my dad?!"

Grace Lorenz was also provoked. Pointing at him, she retorted, "If the Special Operations Division fell into the hands of people like you, it would truly be finished! What's locked up inside are members of the Sterling Family. Have you forgotten what the Special Operations Division's duty is?"

Evelyn's heart sank.

This was bad.

Sure enough, Marcus immediately looked at Grace with anger. "Well, well, Grace. So these are your grandfather's true thoughts, right? I wondered why, for no apparent reason, he didn't arrange for his own son to join but arranged for you instead. Turns out he's been guarding against me all along! I've dedicated my life to the Special Operations Division for so many years, just to be criticized by a junior like you?"

Grace, caught up in the heat of the argument, shot back, "Director Wright, search your conscience. Have you truly given your all to the Special Operations Division?"

Leo sneered. "My father hasn't given his all, but you have? Wandering around the Special Operations Division with your good friend, do you really think this place is your own home?"

Grace wanted to retort, but Benjamin immediately stopped her. "Grace, say less."

As soon as Grace fell silent, Leo scoffed coldly. "Can't argue so you shut up? Grace, Benjamin, stop pretending to be so noble! If you covet the power of the Special Operations Division, just say it outright. Why pretend so convincingly? And that old man Benjamin, he's just an old fox! He's been playing my father for a fool!"

Benjamin was furious. "You can criticize me, but don't insult my father! My father has dedicated himself to the Special Operations Division and has never wronged anyone!"

Leo sneered. "Never wronged anyone? Then why was he captured? I think there must be a problem. Your whole family are spies, right? You should all be arrested!"

Grace exploded with rage. "What did you say? Say that again if you dare!"

Leo taunted, "I said it. What are you going to do about it? Want to fight?"

He rolled up his sleeves.

Grace's little follower's eyes darted. He immediately shouted, "Leo Wright, don't push people too far! Just because our old master is in trouble, you think you can look down on the Wright family? I'll fight you!"

With that, he lowered his head and charged at Leo.

Benjamin tried to stop him but only managed to grab his sleeve.

The man had already rushed in front of Leo.

Leo executed a shoulder throw, slamming him hard to the ground. "You? You think you're worthy of fighting me?"

The little follower took the opportunity to lie on the ground, wailing. "Leo Wright, you went for the kill! You hate the Wright faction this much? I get it now. You want to wipe us out completely, to make the Special Operations Division change its surname to Wright, right?"

Leo was stunned.

He looked down at his hands. He hadn't used much force just now...

Before he could react, another person rushed forward. "Leo Wright, you're going too far! Old Master Wright hasn't even been convicted yet! Is your father that eager to take over? Trying to purge the Special Operations Division?!"

Leo used two more moves, knocking that person to the ground.

Having resorted to violence, his blood was up. Leo arrogantly looked around. "Who else from the Wright faction isn't convinced? Come at me together!"

Not only the people behind Grace, but even Grace herself was provoked. She grabbed a broom and was about to charge forward.

Grace yelled, "Leo Wright, you're absolutely intolerable! I'll beat you to death!"

Zhang Tai, standing behind Leo, immediately shouted, "Charge! Don't let those surnamed Wright bully us!"

A group of people assumed fighting stances. A major brawl was about to erupt.

Benjamin urgently tried to dissuade them. "Everyone, calm down! We need to present a united front today, not fight amongst ourselves!"

Unfortunately, no one was listening.
