Chapter 364

Fiona Valentine curled her lips in a sneer.

"Grace Nan?"

"We already know everything about you before coming here."

Evelyn Ross was about to speak.

Mina Sherwood suddenly rushed forward to shield her.

"Fiona, Penelope, don't go too far!"

"Do you believe I'll tell Marcus Valentine immediately?"

"Let's see how he deals with you!"

Fiona paused, hesitation flashing across her face.

Penelope Valentine lowered her eyes with a soft laugh.

"Big Brother seemed to go out last night."

"It was your wedding night."

"Why weren't you together?"

These words jolted Fiona.

Her eyes lit up.

"Little sister, is that true?"

She rarely used Penelope's full name.

These names always made her feel like mere accessories to the Valentine family.

Penelope nodded.

"I saw it with my own eyes."

"Big Brother left and didn't return all night."

"Sister-in-law, you didn't have a fight, did you?"

"Newlyweds should be inseparable..."

Fiona immediately chimed in.

"It must be because Big Brother doesn't love her at all!"

"There are so many respectable ladies in New York."

"Why would he choose her?"

"She must have used some trick to seduce him!"

"Now she's using her pregnancy to climb up?"

"Mina, I advise you to know your place."

"Do you really think marrying into the Valentine family makes you the lady?"

"Dream on!"

"No one will acknowledge you!"

Penelope spoke gently.

"Fiona, you shouldn't say that."

"She does have the status."

"We should still save face."

Fiona scoffed.

"Saving face is fine."

"Then apologize properly."

"As long as the apology satisfies us, we'll let her go!"

Penelope raised an eyebrow without speaking.

Mina asked foolishly.

"Okay, I'll apologize for Grace."

"Regardless of who was right or wrong before, I'll say sorry."

"Can we all get along peacefully from now on?"

Fiona burst out laughing.

"Mina, are you that naive?"

"Is an apology just with words?"

Mina frowned.

"What do you want then?"

"Money?"

Fiona and Penelope exchanged a glance.

Penelope gave a light laugh.

"We don't need money."

Mina pressed.

"What then?"

"A house?"

"Jewelry?"

"Something else?"

Penelope just looked at Evelyn Ross and smiled without speaking.

Fiona crossed her arms with a cold laugh.

"We won't make it difficult for you, sister-in-law."

"You're pregnant now, so your status is special."

"Just have Grace kowtow to apologize to me."

"Kowtow?"

Mina's voice instantly rose.

"Impossible!"

She finally understood.

She glared at the two angrily.

"You didn't come to negotiate at all!"

"You came to harass Grace!"

"Finally figured it out?" Fiona admitted outright.

"Mina, if you mind your own business now, we won't make trouble for you in the Valentine family."

"Otherwise, you know the consequences!"

"The Valentine family dotes on us the most."

"You won't have a good life!"

Then she looked at Evelyn Ross.

"Kowtow once to me."

"Kowtow twice to my sister."

"How dare you hit her?"

Mina was so angry her face turned pale.

"I said it's impossible!"

"Fiona, leave immediately."

"Otherwise I'll—"

She stopped mid-sentence.

"You'll what?" Fiona sneered.

"Call the police?"

"You're a Valentine now too."

"You should know the police can't do anything to us."

Penelope spoke softly.

"She probably wants to call Big Brother."

"Big Brother?"

"Will Big Brother answer her call?"

"He left on their wedding night."

"Can she order Big Brother around?"

"Besides, if Big Brother were here, who knows who he'd side with!"

Mina was too angry to speak.

"You..."

"What about us?"

Fiona stepped forward.

She pointed her chin at Evelyn Ross.

"Will you kowtow or not?"

"Beg for mercy!"

"Don't force us to do it ourselves!"

Evelyn Ross stood with her arms crossed.

She spoke calmly to Mina and Chloe Roland.

"Go upstairs."

Mina didn't understand.

But Chloe immediately nodded.

She pulled Mina back.

Mina was anxious.

"What are you doing?"

"Grace will get beaten!"

"She won't..."

Chloe reassured her.

"Trust in our savior's strength..."

"What strength?" Mina grew more anxious.

"I grew up with her."

"She'll really lose out!"

"Don't worry, she won't!"

Chloe pulled her hard.

"Don't go over and cause trouble!"

Mina was extremely anxious.

Just as she was about to step forward, she saw Evelyn Ross rotate her wrists.

Evelyn stepped forward and looked at Fiona.

"Enough talk."

"Are you coming together or one by one?"

Fiona was stunned.

Penelope frowned.

Penelope clenched her jaw.

"Grace, forget whether you can beat all these people."

"Even if you can fight, would you dare?"

"Exactly!"

"We are the most beloved daughters of the Valentine family."

"If you dare touch us, the Valentines will never let you go!"

Evelyn tilted her head.

"You talk too much."

"Weren't you just here to pick a fight?"

Fiona looked at Mrs. Lawrence.

"Mrs. Lawrence, aren't you afraid?"

"If she dares resist, your son and her daughter can forget about going to school!"

Mrs. Lawrence said calmly.

"School isn't important."

"What's important is letting Benny know he's not a commodity!"

"Miss Nan, do as you please!"

"At worst, I'll send my son abroad!"

Evelyn glanced at Mrs. Lawrence upon hearing this.

She felt this friend was worth keeping.

She nodded.

At the same time.

Outside the Nan family home.

Luxury cars drove into the neighborhood one after another.

They attracted attention from residents from the moment they entered.

The motorcade stopped directly in front of the Nan house.

Bodyguards quickly got out of the cars.

They jogged to open the car doors.

A handsome middle-aged man in a suit stepped out of the lead Rolls-Royce.

It was Third Master Valentine.

He looked at the small villa.

He noticed the front door was wide open...
