Ginger nodded obediently and spun to grab her smart watch. She couldn’t wait to share the good news with Sage. They might have a father soon!

Wade, who had been watching them, was at a loss for words.

Was this fate or an unfortunate destiny?

First, he was the one who took Ginger to the hotel safely. Second, he was the one who rescued Ginger when someone attempted to kidnap her.

What were the odds? Was it because blood was thicker than water?
