He grabbed a cup of coffee and took a sip, but it didn't help his frustration.

"That's my..."

"I'm different from you. It was just a quick measurement, and it ended quickly! But Ava's still there, you even dare to..."

"Shut up!" Arthur, annoyed,kicked him.

Alan jumped back. "Come on, you don't have to be like this,you already have a lover, and still flirt with women here! If Ella finds out..."

His voice stopped abruptly, feeling a chill on his neck.

"Alright, let's not dwell on those unhappy things! Hey, spending a lot of money to help her deal with bad reviews is no small feat!"

Arthur tilted his head and glanced at him. "She's so clueless, even you

figured it out!"

"Calm down, you're too jealous. You transferred Leo and managed negative comments, What's your plan?"

"Annoying!"

Arthur lifted his eyelids. "Ill talk to Sean another day, and send you to Emberlyn!"

Watching him leave, Alan wailed, "No!"

Wails echoed behind Arthur.

He didn't stop, seeing Leo not far away, his eyes narrowed, his expression unclear.

The live stream ended at noon.

Ella reviewed the playback, seeing that the viewers were focusing on her and Arthur's relationship. She was speechless.

Lily was excited, "It means you two are a perfect match, look, everyone likes you!"

Lily, straightforward, pushed them back. "Oh, no need. You keep it.seriously."
