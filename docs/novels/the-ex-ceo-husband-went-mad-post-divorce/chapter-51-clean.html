Chapter 49

[What are you jealous of? I know you like Ava, but you shouldn't badmouth others. It's just a handshake!]

[Yeah. Ava's fans are all clueless. It's just a handshake, a basic courtesy.I have no idea what nonsense you're talking about!]

Where there were people, there was always drama.

Ava's fans were fiercely defending her, spitting venom with every word.

Ms. Worry-Free's fan base, though small, was scrappy and ready to brawl.

Seeing some fans' dumb comments, they didn't hold back and clapped back hard.

The debate was on.

Ella hadn't even started her work yet, and seeing the comments, she was already feeling overwhelmed.

She was here for a fashion competition, not a brawl, and the fans' behavior would reflect on her.

Ella cleared her throat and waved at the camera. "Hey everyone, I'm Ms.Worry-Free. As a designer, my only goal is to create clothes that everyone loves. Today, I'm gonna show you how to cut fabric."

She pulled out the prepped fabric and showed it to everyone.

"Honestly, tailoring is pretty simple; our grandparents' generation could even do it. First..."

Whether anyone was listening or not, Ella's voice was calm and patient as

she explained the cutting process detailedly.

She spoke while her hands kept cutting.

Soon, the outline of a piece of clothing was almost done.

Lily, standing beside her, kept giving her a thumbs up,showing her appreciation, and handed tools to Ella.

The two worked in perfect sync, creating a realy warm vibe.

The netizens. who were initially arguing,unknowingly stopped and started listening to Ella's explanation.

[Is this the charm of a top designer? Even though I'm not into cutting fabric. I'm willing to listen!]

[Yeah. especially when she talks about the origin and history of the fabric.She's so knowledgeable, a truly intellectual woman. It's a pity we can't see her face!]

[Many of Ava's dumb fans say Ms. Worry-Free is ugly, but just by listeníng to her voice, you can tell she'sdefinitely a beauty!]

Ella didn't care about the comments.

She was just completely lost in her own world.

The door opened, and Ava quietly walked in.

She had changed into a high-end outfit, thinking she would get instant praise from the netizens as soon as she appeared.

Unexpectedly,her arrival was like a drop of water in a lake, not causing any ripples.

The director's team was entirely focused on Ella, not noticing Ava at all.

Anger surged within her, and she quietly approached Ella. Seeing the praised comments only intensified her anger.

"Ms. Worry-Free, you're so fast. Didn't you finish this last night..."

Ava's voice abruptly stopped, as if she had said something wrong, covering her mouth with her hand.

Her fans finally found an opportunity to fight back,and the malicious comments instantly filled the screen.

[I knew it, how could she do it so fast? It's fake. Not only is she ugly, but she's also a cheater!]

[Get out,you disgust me!]

[Ugly woman, get out! Deceiving the audience, ban Ms.Worry-Free!]

The comments quickly scrolled and trended again.

Meanwhile, Arthur was in a video conference when he heard Alan scream beside him.

He shot a cold glance over, and Alan, covering his mouth, handed his phone to Arthur.

#Ms.Worry-Free Cheating

Arthur frowned, "The video conference is over, we'll talk another day!"

He shut down the computer, grabbed Alan's phone. "These people..."

Seeing the abusive comments, he exuded a chilling aura.

Alan chuckled. "Thinking about avenging your girl? Just send a lawyer's letter already!"

Before he could finish. Arthur gave him a light knock on the head.

Luckily, Alan was used to it anad dodged in time, so it didn't hurt. "So,what's the plan now? I'm ready to execute!"

"Mind your own business!"

Arthur turned on his computer and joined to the live stream, watching that determined figure, a smile unconsciously forming on his lips.

In the live stream, the director, knowing how to keep the viewers hooked,made sure both Ava and Ella were in the frame.

Ella. holding the cut fabric, sneered, "You think I finished this last night?"

"Of course not!"

Ava, looking guilty, faced the camera. "Everyone, don't get it twisted. The fabric from last night looks similar to this one, but it's not the same piece!"

Her explanation was even worse than saying nothing.

Lily,furious,jumped up. "Don't talk nonsense. You weren't with us,so how do youa know what happened last night!"

Ava said, "You're righat, I know nothing. Let's continue and not affect our normal work!"

Lily laughed in anger.

Clearly, Ava was the one causing trouble, leading to them being criticized by netizens, and she was still blaming them for affecting the work.

Lily rolled up her sleeves, ready to confront Ava.

Ella grabbed her wrist and shook her head. "All schemes and tricks are powerless in the face of true skill!"

She casually picked up a piece of fabric and stood in front of the camera."

I dislike being misunderstood, so I'll demonstrate it myself. Everyone,see here, this piece of fabric may be small. but it can still be tailored; I just need to adjust the scale."

To make it clear, Ella stood there, letting Lily hold the fabric.

She held scissors in one hand and a ruler in the other. "Just like before,cutting while explaining."

Half an hour later, the new piece of fabric was cut.

Ella placed the two cut pieces together, smiling. "Everyone,see,scaled down. If you don't believe it, we can have the director's team measure it."

Ella shook the two pieces of fabric, making her point clear.

The director's team looked at each other, unwilling to offend Ava.

At this moment, Leo jumped out. "I'll do it, I believe in Ms.Worry-Free!"

He took out a ruler and started measuring,recording as he went.

Ten minutes later, he showed the results to everyone. "Everyone, take a look! Ms. Worry-Free is a true design master. How extraordinary her work is!"

This statement received unanimous praise from the netizens.

Seeing the praise, Ella smiled and bowed.′′It2s my greatest honor to be recognized by everyone!"

With the misunderstanding cleared. Ella didn't dwell on it and continued her work.

After cutting, the next steps were detailing anad sewing.

The show had all the tools. Seeing the pearls, Ella came up with a good idea.

She found a gold thread, strung the pearls, and sewed them onto the neckline.

Lily, in sync with her, saw comments asking questions about Ella's action and answered directly, "Compared to diamonds, this evening gown looks even more elegant with pearl embellishments."

[Watching the live stream is rewarding, enhancing my taste!]

[Thank you for teaching me how to match1,I love it!]

Ava's fans still disagreed, but their insulting comments were quickly replaced by the praise.

For a moment, Ava was furious but helpless, and to let the netizens forget her words, she could only shrink into a corner.
